About 149FM

One person, one goal: non-stop music, no ad breaks that ruin the mood, no playlists dictated by a corporate algorithm.

149FM is built from scratch — own server, own automation, everything learned along the way. It's not a corporate station; it's a passion project that broadcasts 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

Our identity is the lack of a label: one night it's Drum & Bass at 3am, the next it's Jazz at noon and Nu Metal on Saturday. If it sounds good, it belongs here.
